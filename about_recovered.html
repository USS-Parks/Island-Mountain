About Us

Built in Colorado. Shipped Ready to Run.

Island Mountain builds on-premise AI inference servers for organizations that cannot -- or will not -- send their data to the cloud. Every system is assembled, configured, burn-tested, and shipped directly to your facility.

The Company

Why Island Mountain Exists

AI is changing how every industry operates. But for organizations handling privileged, classified, or sovereignty-protected data, the cloud is not an option. Attorney-client communications, patient health records, ITAR-controlled defense data, and tribal governance information all carry legal obligations that cloud AI services cannot satisfy.

Island Mountain was founded to solve that problem directly: pre-built, burn-tested GPU inference servers that arrive at your facility ready to run. No cloud accounts. No API keys. No per-token billing. No data leaving your building.

We build three tiers of hardware -- from a $75,000 Starter system with dual NVIDIA H100 GPUs to a $350,000+ Premium configuration with H200 SXM modules pushing 282GB of VRAM. Every system ships with DeepSeek V4-Flash pre-installed, OpenWebUI configured for your team, and 72 hours of burn-in testing completed before it leaves Colorado.

Leadership

Who Builds Your System

Island Mountain is a Colorado LLC founded by John Dougherty. This is a one-person builder operation by design. When you buy a system, the person who specced it, assembled it, configured the software, ran the burn-in tests, and packed it for shipping is the same person who answers your phone call six months later when you have a question.

That is not a limitation. It is the product. You are not buying from a channel partner or a reseller with a markup and a help desk. You are buying from the builder, with direct access to the person who knows your exact configuration.

Our Approach

What Makes This Different

72-Hour Burn-In Testing

Every system runs continuous inference workloads for 72 hours before shipping. Thermal behavior, memory stability, and GPU performance are validated under sustained load. If a component is going to fail, it fails on our bench, not yours.

Air-Gap Ready

Systems are configured to operate with zero internet connectivity. Models, software, and updates are pre-loaded. Your data never needs to leave your network for any reason.

One-Time Purchase

No subscriptions. No per-seat licensing. No per-token API charges. You buy the hardware, you own the hardware. Ongoing costs are limited to electricity and your own IT staff time.

Direct Builder Support

Support comes from the person who built your system. No ticket queues, no tier-one scripts, no escalation chains. You call, you get the builder. That is the model.

Markets Served

Who We Build For

Island Mountain serves ten primary markets, each with distinct regulatory or sovereignty requirements that make cloud AI untenable:

Law Firms -- Attorney-client privilege under ABA Model Rule 1.6 creates an affirmative duty to protect client data. Cloud AI services cannot guarantee that obligation is met. Learn more about local AI for law firms.

Medical Practices -- HIPAA compliance without a Business Associate Agreement. Your patient data stays on your hardware, in your building. Learn more about HIPAA-compliant AI.

Defense Contractors -- ITAR, CUI, and CMMC-regulated data cannot touch commercial cloud infrastructure. Air-gapped local inference is the only compliant path. Learn more about ITAR-compliant AI hardware.

Tribal Nations -- Data sovereignty is not a feature request. It is a right. OCAP principles (Ownership, Control, Access, Possession) require that tribal data remain under tribal control. Learn more about tribal data sovereignty AI.

Research Labs -- Unpublished findings, grant-funded datasets, and pre-patent IP need to stay off commercial servers. Learn more about local AI for research labs.

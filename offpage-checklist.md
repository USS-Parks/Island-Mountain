## 3. Podcast Pitch Templates

### Legal Tech Podcasts
**Targets:** LawSites, Artificial Lawyer, Legal Talk Network, Technically Legal

**Pitch:**
Subject: Guest pitch — Attorney-client privilege is structurally incompatible with cloud AI

John Dougherty builds on-premise AI servers for law firms. His argument: when you send client data to ChatGPT or Claude, you've created a third-party record that opposing counsel can subpoena. No NDA fixes this. No terms of service fixes this. The only structural solution is hardware you own, running models you control, inside your building. He's shipped systems to firms handling thousands of contracts per year. Happy to discuss the specific privilege mechanics, the ABA's position, and what "local AI" actually looks like in a law office.

### Healthcare IT Podcasts
**Targets:** Healthcare IT News Podcast, HIT Like a Girl, Health IT on the Record

**Pitch:**
Subject: Guest pitch — HIPAA technical safeguards and AI: why cloud solutions fail the audit

The HIPAA Security Rule requires specific technical safeguards for any system processing PHI. Cloud AI services create gaps in access controls, audit logging, and transmission security that no BAA fully addresses. John Dougherty builds on-premise AI servers that satisfy HIPAA technical safeguards structurally — PHI never leaves the building. Happy to walk through the specific 45 CFR 164.312 requirements and how local hardware maps to each one.

### Tribal Governance / Sovereignty Podcasts
**Targets:** Tribal Business News, The Sovereign Tech, Native America Calling

**Pitch:**
Subject: Guest pitch — OCAP principles require sovereign AI infrastructure

The CLOUD Act gives U.S. law enforcement the power to compel cloud providers to produce data stored anywhere. For tribal nations committed to OCAP principles — Ownership, Control, Access, Possession — cloud AI structurally violates the Possession requirement. John Dougherty builds on-premise AI servers deployed on tribal land, and has worked directly with tribal emergency management and health services departments. Happy to discuss the intersection of data sovereignty, the CLOUD Act, and practical AI deployment for tribal governments.

### Defense / Government Podcasts
**Targets:** Defense One Podcasts, GovExec Daily, Federal News Network

**Pitch:**
Subject: Guest pitch — ITAR and DFARS compliance for AI: why cloud providers are a supply chain risk

Defense contractors processing CUI or ITAR-controlled data cannot send that data to cloud AI providers without creating compliance gaps that no contract can close. John Dougherty builds air-gapped AI inference servers with NVIDIA H100 GPUs for defense subcontractors. Happy to discuss CMMC alignment, DFARS 252.204-7012 requirements, and what audit-ready AI infrastructure looks like.

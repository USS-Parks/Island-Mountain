How It Works

Conversation

Tell us what you need: workload type, number of users, data sensitivity requirements. We recommend the right tier.

Quote & Deposit

Your Protection

Warranty & Buyer Protections

1-Year Hardware Warranty

Full coverage on all components. If something fails within the first year, we fix or replace it. Supplier RMA agreements back every component.

14-Day Price Lock

GPU markets fluctuate. Your quote is locked for 14 days from acceptance. If GPU prices spike above 10%, you can cancel with a full deposit refund.

30-Day Setup Support

Direct phone and email access to the builder for the first 30 days. Network configuration, user setup, model questions -- whatever you need to get running.

When you are ready for more performance, your existing GPUs are credited at secondary market value toward the next-generation cards.

Pricing Questions

Common Pricing Questions

Does Island Mountain offer financing or payment plans?

We do not currently offer direct financing. However, many buyers finance through equipment lenders who treat AI servers like any other capital equipment purchase. Companies like Balboa Capital and LEAF Commercial Capital offer terms from 24-60 months. During the quote process, we can point you toward lenders who work with technology equipment.

Can I write off local AI hardware as a business expense?

In most cases, yes. AI inference hardware qualifies as capital equipment and is typically eligible for Section 179 deduction or MACRS depreciation. Section 179 may allow you to deduct the full purchase price in the year of acquisition. Consult your CPA for guidance specific to your situation. Island Mountain is not a tax advisor.

What if GPU prices drop after I place my order?

Your quote is locked for 14 days from acceptance. If prices drop after you commit, the quoted price stands. The GPU secondary market fluctuates daily, and the 14-day lock protects both sides. If you expect a significant price drop, you can wait to request a quote until you are ready.

Is the 1-year warranty included or extra?

Included in every system price. No additional charge for baseline warranty or 30-day setup support. Extended warranty options beyond year one are available at purchase. The warranty is backed by a 20% reserve per unit, meaning replacement funds are set aside at the time of your purchase.

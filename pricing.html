Risk Management

Cash Flow & Risk Controls

50% Deposit Policy

Buyer funds cover component procurement. The business never floats more than $3,000-$7,000 in working capital per build. No inventory risk on speculative builds.

GPU Price Volatility Clause

14-day price lock from quote acceptance. Price increases above 15% allow mutual cancellation. Buyer cancellation right above 10% increase. Protects both parties from market swings.

20% Warranty Reserve

20% of hardware cost set aside per unit for warranty obligations. Covers component failure within the 1-year warranty period. Supplier RMA agreements serve as primary failure mitigation.

Positive from Unit One

Deposit policy means every completed sale generates positive cash flow. No break-even volume required. The business is profitable on the first unit sold.

For Investors

Investment & Business Fundamentals

Tangible Collateral

Each unit contains $28,000-$35,000 in NVIDIA GPUs and enterprise hardware with active secondary market resale value. GPUs are depreciable assets with documented market pricing.

5-Year MACRS Depreciation

Computing equipment qualifies for 5-year MACRS depreciation. Section 179 deduction may apply for buyers. Accelerated depreciation provides tax advantage for purchasers.

Cash Flow Positive Immediately

50% deposit policy means the business generates positive cash flow from the first completed sale. No minimum volume required to break even. Working capital requirement per build: $3,000-$7,000.

Business Formation

Colorado LLC with EIN, Sales Tax License, and general liability + professional liability insurance. Proper business formation and tax compliance from day one.

Structural Market Demand

Data sovereignty demand is driven by regulatory requirements (HIPAA, ITAR, attorney-client privilege), not market trends. This demand is structural and growing as AI adoption increases.

36% Gross Margin

Healthy margin structure at midpoint pricing with built-in warranty reserve. No recurring costs eat into margins. Every unit sold contributes directly to the bottom line.

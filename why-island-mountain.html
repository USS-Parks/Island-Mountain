The Case for Local AI

Why Local AI Hardware?

Every token you send to a cloud API is data you've handed to someone else. For some organizations, that's a risk that can't be managed with terms of service.

The Problem

Cloud LLM Subscriptions Are a Recurring Liability

Perpetual Per-Token Billing

Cloud LLM subscriptions cost $20-$200 per user per month with per-token billing on top. At 10 users, that's $2,400-$24,000 per year -- recurring, forever, with prices that only go up.

Shared Processing Environments

Your prompts, your data, your client information -- all processed on shared infrastructure you don't control. Cloud providers' privacy policies aren't your privacy policies.

Zero Model Control

Cloud providers can change models, alter behavior, restrict access, or discontinue services at any time. Your workflows break when their priorities shift.

The Solution

One-Time Investment. Permanent Capability.

Complete Data Sovereignty

DeepSeek R1 70B and Llama 3.1 70B running on hardware you own, in a building you control. No data leaves your premises. No exceptions.

Unlimited Inference

Run as many prompts as you want, for as many users as you need, at zero marginal cost. The only cost after purchase is electricity.

MIT Licensed Models

You own the models outright. No vendor lock-in. No licensing fees. No usage restrictions. Run them, modify them, keep them forever.

Our Buyers

Who We Serve

Organizations where data sovereignty isn't a nice-to-have -- it's a business requirement.

Boutique AI Consulting

Consultancies that need to run client data through LLMs without exposing it to cloud providers. Your competitive advantage is confidentiality.

Law Firms

Attorney-client privilege doesn't survive a cloud API call. Contract analysis, case research, and document review need to stay in-house.

Medical Practices

Patient data processed locally eliminates an entire category of HIPAA exposure. No BAAs needed when data never leaves your network.

Research Labs

Grant-funded research with proprietary data, intellectual property concerns, and publication timelines that can't depend on third-party uptime.

Small Defense Contractors

ITAR, CUI, and classified-adjacent work requires air-gapped or locally-controlled processing. Cloud inference is structurally incompatible.

Local Government & Tribal Nations

Constituent data, emergency management information, and internal communications that need to stay under sovereign jurisdiction.

Honest Positioning

What We're Not

We don't compete with Lambda Labs or Dell for enterprise SOC 2 contracts. We don't offer onsite support teams or 24/7 NOC monitoring. We don't have a compliance department or a procurement portal.

What we build: boutique, personally-delivered AI hardware for organizations that value data privacy and personal service over vendor compliance paperwork. Every system is assembled, tested, and delivered by the person who answers your phone call.

If you need SOC 2 Type II and an SLA with 99.99% uptime guarantees, Lambda or Dell is your vendor. If you need a system that works, a person who picks up the phone, and the confidence that your data never leaves your control -- that's what we do.

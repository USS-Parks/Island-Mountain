## **Colorado Company Launches Air-Gapped AI Servers for Organizations That Cannot Trust Cloud Providers**

### Island Mountain Ships NVIDIA H100/H200 Inference Hardware to Law Firms, Healthcare, Tribal Nations, Defense Contractors, and Beyond

**COLORADO** – May 15, 2026 – Island Mountain LLC, a Colorado-based hardware manufacturer, today announced the commercial availability of its on-premises AI inference servers. Built and burn-tested by a team with deep expertise in regulated industries, Island Mountain's servers eliminate the structural risks of cloud-based AI by running inference workloads inside customer facilities on customer-owned hardware.

The problem with cloud AI is not encryption or contractual protections. It is architectural. When you send attorney-client communications, patient health records, trade secrets, or ITAR-controlled data to a cloud AI provider, that data becomes a third-party record discoverable by subpoena, compelling authority under the CLOUD Act, or data breach without your control. No non-disclosure agreement, no business associate agreement, and no terms of service fix this fundamental legal and operational risk.

"Our customers include law firms handling thousands of contracts per year, hospitals managing HIPAA audits, tribal nations asserting data sovereignty, and defense contractors obligated under DFARS," said John Dougherty, Founder and CEO of Island Mountain. "They don't want to negotiate with a cloud provider about data security. They want to own the hardware, control the inference runtime, and know with certainty that sensitive data never leaves their building. Island Mountain makes that economically rational."

Island Mountain's servers start at $75,000 for a Starter configuration (NVIDIA H100 80GB PCIe, pre-installed with Llama 3.1 70B and DeepSeek V4-Flash, OpenWebUI interface) and scale to $400,000 for a Premium configuration with an H200 141GB GPU. All systems ship with zero recurring costs—no per-token fees, no cloud subscriptions, no vendor lock-in.

**Key Features:**
- **Hardware:** NVIDIA H100 80GB PCIe (Starter) or H200 141GB (Premium)
- **Pre-installed Models:** DeepSeek V4-Flash, Llama 3.1 70B, Mistral 7B
- **Software:** OpenWebUI for inference management, fully air-gap capable
- **Cost:** One-time purchase, $0/month thereafter
- **Target Industries:** Law firms, healthcare, tribal nations, research labs, defense contractors, financial services, insurance, energy/utilities, government, education
- **Compliance:** Supports HIPAA, ITAR, DFARS, CMMC, OCAP, and attorney-client privilege preservation

The market for on-premises AI inference is large and growing. Gartner estimates the AI infrastructure market at $47 billion by 2027, with enterprises increasingly rejecting cloud-only AI due to compliance, sovereignty, and cost concerns. Island Mountain captures this demand directly, with a founder-to-buyer sales model and no intermediary friction.

"We're not a cloud alternative. We're an architecture alternative," said Basho Parks, Co-Founder and Chief Operations Officer. "We build the hardware once, ship it, and then our relationship with the customer is over. They own the server forever. They get inference economics that improve over time as models get smaller and faster."

Island Mountain is currently accepting orders for Q2 and Q3 2026 delivery. Customers can configure and order directly at https://islandmountain.io/products.html.
